Enhanced Validated Public Library

An Example of a Validated JavaScript Front-End App Using localStorage

    •

    The app supports the four standard data management operations (Create/Read/Update/Delete). For playing with the app, you may first want to and then view it (e.g. by choosing "Manage books" and then "List all books").

    This example app takes care of two class hierarchies:

    • The simple segmentation of Book into TextBook and Biography.
    • The segmentation of Person into Author and Employee such that Employee has a further segment, Manager.
